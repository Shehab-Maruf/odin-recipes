Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • Oil/Ghee
  • Onions
  • Bone-in, cut up, skinless chicken
  • Whole spices:
  • Garlic + Ginger
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Rice
  • Dried Plums (Alu Bukhara):

how to cook in 3 steps

  1. Prepare the chicken curry. Prepare the biryani masala (or use store-bought) and marinate the chicken. Cook the chicken curry. While it's cooking over low heat, prepare the rice.
  2. Parboil the rice. Bring a pot of water to a boil and parboil the rice. Drain and set aside.
  3. Bring it all together for a final steam ('dum'). Allow steam to develop, then lower the heat and let the flavors meld.
